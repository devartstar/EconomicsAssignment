INTERNATIONAL TRADE

Before jumping to my problem statement for the assignment I first want to discuss the topic of International Trade. The question of importance is that, if a country has the capability of producing goods by itself then why should it be involved in International Trade? The main reason is that trade promotes specialization. If a country is exporting some good then it's obvious that it should have expertise in producing that good. This may include that their production cost may be less or they may have abundant raw materials or labor supply for the production of that good. Hence specialization promotes Productivity, Employment, and overall enhancement in the Standard of Living in the long run.

David Ricardo in 1870

In his article on (Principle of political economy and Taxation). He said that if countries trade based on specialization and free trade then it would be profitable to all the nations even for the relatively inefficient country. (as the less efficient countries can focus on specialization). Giving rise to the principle of Comparative and Absolute Advantage.

Absolute Advantage(Directly comparing actual cost)

Absolute advantage can be a result of a country's natural endowment. (or the country exports those substances which it has in abundance and imports those substances which are in scarcity ) .

Comparative Advantage(Comparing the opportunity Cost)

Opportunity cost means how much the production of the other product gets affected by the production of one product. So a country must import those goods whose opportunity cost as compared to other products is more and export those which opportunity cost is less as compared to the other trading partner. Then the welfare of both will increase.

Example -

Consider an example wherein International Trade two countries are participating and there are two items in the market ( CLOTH and BOOKS)

COUNTRY CLOTH BOOKS

France 20 20 In one labor hour, the USA produces 20 cloth OR 20 books

Now consider if both nations focus equally on both goods so France will produce 2.5 units of cloth and 5 units of Books giving a total production of 7.5 units of France similarly the USA will produce 10 units of cloth and 10 units of Book giving a total production of 20 units of USA.

Hence the total production of both is 27.5 units of goods. But is it the maximum both can produce in total?

The answer is NO! If we calculate the opportunity cost

Opportunity cost simply meant the amount of other good one has to sacrifice to produce 1 unit of a good.

COUNTRY Oppurtunity Cost(cloth) Oppurtunity Cost(books)
France 5/2.5 = 2 2.5/5 = 0.5 So France specializes in books
France 10/10 = 1 10/10 = 1 So the USA specializes in cloth ( for better trade)

So now taking account of specialization in 1 labor hour FRANCE produces 10 units of book and USA produces 20 units of cloth hence the total production is 30 units(Comparative Advantage) of goods which are better than the previous 27.5 units. (Absolute Advantage).

So there is an overall enhancement of all countries so all countries try to participate in International Trade.
