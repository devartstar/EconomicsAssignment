Before jumping to my problem statement for the assignment I first want to discuss the topic of International Trade.
The question of importance is that, if a country has the capability of producing goods by itself then why should it be involved in International Trade?
The main reason is that trade promotes specialization. If a country is exporting some good then it's obvious that it should have expertise in producing that good. This may include that their production cost may be less or they may have abundant raw materials or labor supply for the production of that good.
